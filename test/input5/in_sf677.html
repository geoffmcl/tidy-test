After that first moment of shock, his arms came round me and his beautiful hands were buried in my hair, and it felt like… well, I don’t know what it felt like. Like that feeling you get when you’re reading a mystery novel and you figure out whodunnit before the characters do. Like that feeling you get when you work through a complicated Arithmancy proof - and then you look up the answer in the back of the textbook and it’s the right one.
