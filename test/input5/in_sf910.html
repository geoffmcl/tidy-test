Three dead in apparent murder-suicide in south-central Kansas

BY RON SYLVESTER

The Wichita Eagle

More News

A pending divorce turned deadly Sunday night, as McPherson County authorities say a man shot and killed his estranged wife and a friend before turning the gun on himself.

McPherson County Sheriff Larry Powell said today that Becky R. Huff, 33, and Brian Huff, 32, hadn't finalized their divorce when they were found dead at a home near Canton in an apparent murder-suicide.

Also dead is Mark Luttig, 47, a friend of Becky Huff's.

Powell said Becky Huff's two children, ages 13 and 6, witnessed the killings. They were not injured.

Authorities received a call from the house at 2653 Frontier Road just after 10 p.m. Sunday, Powell said.

The bodies of Becky Huff and Luttig were found inside, Powell said.

Authorities found Brian Huff outside his vehicle west of the residence, with an apparent self-inflicted gunshot wound.

Powell is awaiting autopsy results from the Sedgwick County Forensic Science Center.

The two children are being cared for by relatives, Powell said.
