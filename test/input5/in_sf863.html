In Cape Coral’s beginning, a sack of flour marked your spot in the Cape. Today, when you become a risk-free Priority Member, you’re entitled to the best selection of residence style and location selection and so much more, including:

• Preferential pricing
• Guaranteed access to a full continuum of care
• Invitations to special Members-only events and engagements
• Update on the latest community news and information
• Charter Member Benefits

There’s no obligation to becoming a member. Contact us to receive your Priority Registration Card, which you can complete and return to receive a Priority Number that ensures you’re among the first to select a residence style and location at Cape View.

Once architectural plans and pricing are finalized, you’ll be invited, in order of Priority Number, to schedule an appointment at our Information Center. Only when you’ve selected and reserved a residence will you be asked to make a refundable deposit equal to 10% of the entrance fee for the residence you select.

What are you waiting for? Start enjoying the privileges of membership today! Request your Priority Registration Card.
